The official meme token — ShutUpCoin (SHUT)

About SHUT

“Shut up and take my money!” is the iconic meme moment from Futurama where Fry throws cash at something he wants. $SHUT (ShutUpCoin) is a light-hearted memecoin inspired by that exact feeling — viral, absurd and community-driven. This project is for fun, memes and community events.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 SHUT
  • Taxes (Buy/Sell): 5% total (3% to liquidity, 1% marketing, 1% community rewards)

How to Buy

  1. Connect your crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask).
  2. Go to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap/PancakeSwap or PumpFun.
  3. Add the $SHUT token contract (coming soon) and purchase $SHUT.
  4. Add $SHUT to your wallet as a custom token to view your balance.

Comment acheter

  1. Connectez votre wallet crypto (ex. MetaMask).
