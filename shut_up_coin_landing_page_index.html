About SHUT

“Shut up and take my money!” is a popular meme featuring Fry from Futurama throwing cash. It perfectly captures the moment you see something so awesome that you just want to buy it immediately. $SHUT (ShutUpCoin) is our tongue-in-cheek memecoin tribute to that frenzy. It's a humorous, viral, community-driven token with an absurd spirit – 100% for fun, with no serious profit promises.

À propos de SHUT

La phrase « Shut up and take my money! » vient d’un épisode de Futurama où Fry brandit de l’argent. Ce meme exprime l’enthousiasme maximal de l'acheteur, prêt à tout pour avoir le produit. Le token $SHUT est un memecoin décalé inspiré de ce moment, humoristique et communautaire. C’est 100 % pour le fun, sans promesse sérieuse de gain.

How to Buy

  1. Connect your crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask).
  2. Go to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap/PancakeSwap.
  3. Add the $SHUT token contract (coming soon) and purchase $SHUT.
  4. Add $SHUT to your wallet as a custom token to view your balance.
